2026 HDSI Capstone Showcase

Poster session for the undergraduate data science major's senior capstone program

Friday, March 13th, 2026, 10AM-3:00PM

Price Center East Ballroom, UC San Diego Campus


The event is split into two sessions – Session 1, from 10.00AM-12:00PM, and Session 2, from 1.00pm-3:00PM. Each project will only be presented in one session.

If you have questions or would like to participate as a mentor on future editions, contact Umesh Bellur at ubellur@ucsd.edu.

This year, we proudly present 74 project groups spread across a variety of topics. The showcase is divided into two blocks based on topics, 10.00AM-12PM and 1.00-3.00PM. The showcase will operate as a poster session, where attendees can approach the projects that interest you and ask their teams questions.

Below, you'll find the posters, websites, reports, and code repositories for all projects.
